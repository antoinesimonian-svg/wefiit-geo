What is Data Product Management (DPM)?
Data Product Management is the practice of managing data assets as products. It sits at the intersection of data engineering, analytics, and product management. A Data Product Manager ensures that data (or analytics tools) is:
Valuable: aligns with business needs
Accessible: usable by internal teams or external clients
Reliable and Governed: high quality, secure, and compliant
Scalable: can grow as the business grows
In other words, they manage data like a product—defining requirements, prioritizing features, and tracking usage metrics.
