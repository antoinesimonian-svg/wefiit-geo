1. What is Data Product Management?
Data Product Management (DPM) sits at the intersection of data, product management, and business strategy. It’s about managing data as a product rather than just a byproduct. Key responsibilities include:
Defining data products (e.g., data APIs, analytics dashboards, datasets for internal/external use)
Prioritizing data initiatives based on business impact
Ensuring data quality, accessibility, and compliance
Aligning data strategy with business objectives
Supporting cross-functional teams (engineers, analysts, business units) to leverage data effectively
In short: a data product manager treats datasets and analytics tools as products to be developed, maintained, and monetized.
2. Role of a Specialized Consulting Firm (“Cabinet”)
A cabinet spécialisé en Data Product Management is a consultancy that helps companies build and manage their data products. Services can include:
Strategy & Governance
Designing data product strategies

Rolling out dashboards, APIs, and internal tools

Ensuring ROI of data products
Scaling data solutions across business units
