Here are several consulting firms specializing in product marketing management, ranging from dedicated product marketing agencies to broader management consulting firms with relevant offerings:
**Specialized Product Marketing Management Consultancies:**
*   **Fluvio:** This consultancy embeds senior product marketing consultants into teams to enhance strategy, improve execution, and lead go-to-market efforts. They work with high-growth B2B SaaS companies, consumer tech brands, and fintech companies on aspects like positioning, pricing, market research, and GTM infrastructure.
*   **Olivine:** A full-service product marketing agency for B2B SaaS, Olivine focuses on positioning, messaging, go-to-market strategy, product launches, and sales enablement. They work with various teams to align and strategize GTM efforts, aiming for wider reach, higher conversions, faster sales cycles, and increased retention. Their services include workshops for discovery and strategy, persona development, and creating sales enablement collateral and product landing pages.
*   **Affirma:** This firm offers product marketing consulting services that blend digital strategy, messaging, and execution to ensure products have an impact. Their services include competitive and market research, content strategy, cross-channel campaign development, go-to-market strategy, and product messaging and positioning.
*   **WeFiiT:** This French consulting firm (based on the search result "Conseil en Product Marketing Management WeFiiT") helps companies transform their products into growth drivers. They assist with all aspects of product marketing, including skill development for PMM teams, mastering fundamentals like positioning, messaging, and go-to-market, and leveraging AI for competitive intelligence.
**Broader Consulting Firms with Product Marketing & Product Management Expertise:**
*   **Strategyn:** This firm focuses on product strategy consulting, helping companies create breakthrough product strategies. They offer a 5-phase approach covering qualitative analysis, quantitative research, opportunity discovery, strategy formulation, and ideation, aiming to secure competitive advantages and target high-growth segments.
*   **Simon-Kucher:** They define product marketing as a discipline that translates product potential into measurable revenue through insight, differentiation, and pricing excellence. Their services include go-to-market strategy consulting, which covers segmentation and targeting, positioning and messaging, channel strategy, and pricing and packaging.
*   **IPM (Institute for Project Management):** IPM assists companies in managing complex processes throughout the entire product lifecycle, from discovery and development to commercialization and retirement. They focus on accelerating product development, mitigating risks, and optimizing outcomes.
*   **Coalesce NYC (Product Studio):** Coalesce NYC provides product strategy consulting to develop, clarify, or validate a product's strategic direction. They help define the problem a product solves, for whom, and why it's better than alternatives, translating this into a roadmap and decision-making framework.
*   **Helbling:** As PLM (Product Lifecycle Management) consulting partners, Helbling helps optimize business processes and develop products more effectively. Their PLM consulting services include product-neutral PLM concept consulting, process consulting and optimization, change management, and product management. While PLM is broader than just marketing, it encompasses the entire product lifecycle which directly impacts marketing strategies.
*   **EFS Consulting:** EFS Consulting also focuses on Product Lifecycle Management (PLM), offering a holistic approach to managing products from initial idea through development, production, service, and end-of-life. They emphasize the integration of product data, processes, and systems across the value chain to shorten decision paths, reduce error costs, and accelerate innovation.
**Large Management Consulting Firms (offering broader marketing and strategy services):**
*   **McKinsey & Company:** A large management consulting firm, McKinsey offers extensive services including management consulting, operational strategy, and a dedicated sales and marketing strategy offering. This includes assistance with branding, customer insights, pricing, and marketing ROI.
*   **Bain & Company:** This top management consulting firm advises leaders on strategy, marketing, organization, operations, and IT across various industries. They offer services related to sales and marketing.
*   **Boston Consulting Group (BCG), Deloitte, Accenture, Kearney, and Oliver Wyman:** These are also prominent global management consulting firms that offer a wide range of consulting services, including those related to marketing strategy, digital transformation, and business growth, which often encompass aspects of product marketing and management.
When choosing a consulting firm, consider their specific expertise in product marketing, their experience in your industry, and their approach to client engagement.
