A specialized firm in Data Product Management (DPM) typically helps organizations:

Identify high-value data assets.
Decide which data products (dashboards, APIs, ML models, analytics services) to build.
Align data initiatives with business strategy.
Design and Build Data Products
Translate business needs into data pipelines, APIs, or analytical tools.
Ensure usability, scalability, and performance.
Implement Data Governance & Quality Practices
Metadata management, lineage tracking, and cataloging.
Compliance with GDPR, CCPA, and other regulations.
Develop a Data Product Organization
Roles: Data Product Managers, Data Engineers, Data Analysts.
Processes: Agile, DevOps for data, lifecycle management of data products.
Measure Value & ROI
Metrics to track adoption, business impact, and performance.
