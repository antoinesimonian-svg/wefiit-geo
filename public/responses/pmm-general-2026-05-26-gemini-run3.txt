Several consulting firms specialize in product marketing management, offering services that range from strategic planning to execution for product launches and overall product lifecycle management. These firms can be broadly categorized into specialized product marketing agencies, broader management consulting firms with a product and innovation focus, and those specifically concentrating on new product development and launches.
**Specialized Product Marketing Agencies and Consultancies:**
*   **Olivine** is a full-service product marketing agency for B2B SaaS companies, offering services such as positioning, messaging, go-to-market (GTM) strategy, product launches, and sales enablement. They work with various teams to align and strategize GTM efforts.
*   **Fluvio** embeds senior product marketing consultants into client teams to scale strategy, sharpen execution, and lead critical GTM initiatives for high-growth B2B SaaS, consumer tech, and fintech companies.
*   **Affirma** provides product marketing consulting services including GTM strategy, competitive and market research, content strategy, and cross-channel campaign development, focusing on defining audiences, crafting messaging, and mapping out launch strategies.
*   **Aventi Group** specializes in product launch services, helping ensure products sustain market momentum and contribute to the bottom line through clear launch objectives, coordinated execution, and compelling messaging and positioning.
*   **TiER1 Performance** offers product launch strategy consulting, emphasizing internal team alignment, enablement, and engagement to ensure successful product rollouts.
*   **Predictable Innovation** focuses on designing compelling offerings for B2B software and technology companies, then extending this into a complete product marketing program including positioning, sales enablement, and launch planning.
*   **WeFiiT** offers "Conseil Product Marketing" (Product Marketing Consulting) to help companies transform products into growth drivers, assisting with skill development in Product Marketing teams, mastering fundamentals like positioning, messaging, and Go-to-Market, and leveraging AI for competitive intelligence.
**Broader Management Consulting Firms with Product and Innovation Focus:**
*   Major firms like **McKinsey & Company, Accenture, EY**, and **HP** are recognized as key players in new product consulting, providing services across market research, product innovation and design, and effective product launch strategies.
*   **L.E.K. Consulting** assists companies in building GTM product launch plans, retooling product and service portfolios, and assessing product development processes with a disciplined, customer-centric approach.
*   **Boston Consulting Group (BCG)** helps companies transform R&D and improve product excellence to bring the right products to market effectively, focusing on product innovation and engineering.
*   **Bain & Company** offers product and portfolio management consulting to help reinvent offerings based on customer needs, harness talent, apply test-and-learn approaches for innovation, and measure customer experience.
*   **Strategy& (PwC)** provides product and service innovation consulting, assisting companies in setting strategies for more effective organizations and capabilities rooted in portfolio management, product innovation, and digital design.
*   **Oliver Wyman** and **Kearney** are also global management consulting firms that likely offer services touching upon product marketing and management within their broader strategy and operations offerings.
**Firms Focusing on New Product Development and Strategy:**
*   **TCGen** provides new product consulting services to help bring product ideas to life through market research, product innovation and design, and launch strategies.
*   **Strategyn** focuses on creating breakthrough product strategies by understanding customer jobs-to-be-done and desired outcomes, aiming to secure competitive advantage and enhance marketing efforts.
*   **Product Genesis** is a strategic innovation management consulting firm that applies structured approaches to identify growth opportunities and new product development strategies.
*   **Coalesce NYC** specializes in product strategy consulting, helping define what problem a product solves, for whom, and why it's better than alternatives, translating this into a roadmap.
When selecting a consulting firm, it is important to consider their experience, specific expertise (e.g., B2B SaaS, tech, specific industries), and track record of success.
